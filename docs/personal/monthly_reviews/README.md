# Monthly Reviews

## Purpose
Strategic checkpoint to:
- Assess progress against monthly objectives
- Measure progress on BCI goals (3/6/12-month milestones)
- Measure progress on career plan milestones
- Course-correct and adjust plans
- Generate reports for leadership and performance tracking

## Format
- One file per month: `YYYY_MM_review.md` (e.g., `2025_10_review.md`)
- 1-2 hours on last day of month
- Review 4-5 weekly reviews from the month
- Connect to strategic goals

## Usage
- Review all weekly reviews from the month
- Assess progress against monthly objectives in both goal systems
- Calculate metrics (time distribution, delegation success rate, etc.)
- Identify what's working and what needs adjustment
- Plan next month's priorities
- **Generate reports** for leadership, performance reviews, portfolio

## Why Monthly Reviews Matter
- **Strategic checkpoint** - ensures you're on track
- **Course correction** - catch problems before they compound
- **Report generation** - all your accomplishments summarized for multiple audiences
- **Pattern recognition** - see trends over 4 weeks
