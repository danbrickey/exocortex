## Daily Workflow (10-15 min/day)

1. Create today's journal: `personal/daily_journal/YYYY_MM_DD.md`
2. Capture:
   - Meeting notes
   - Delegations (with fear tracking!)
   - Work completed
   - Tomorrow's to-dos
   - Reflections

**Template**: `personal/daily_journal/template.md`

---

## Weekly Workflow (30-45 min, Fridays)

1. Create weekly review: `personal/weekly_reviews/YYYY_Wxx_review.md`
2. Review this week's daily journals
3. Summarize accomplishments
4. Track delegation progress
5. Assess BCI goals and career plan progress
6. Plan next week

**Template**: `personal/weekly_reviews/template.md`

---

## Monthly Workflow (1-2 hours, end of month)

1. Create monthly review: `personal/monthly_reviews/YYYY_MM_review.md`
2. Review this month's weekly reviews
3. Calculate metrics and assess goals
4. **Generate reports**:
   - BCI leadership summary (10 min)
   - Update accomplishment log (5 min)
   - Update career portfolio (as needed)

**Template**: `personal/monthly_reviews/template.md`

## Why This System Works

### ‚úÖ No Duplication
You write once in your daily journal. Everything else is synthesis and reporting from that single source of truth.

### ‚úÖ Integrated Progress Tracking
BCI goals and career goals are tracked together. Same weekly/monthly review process for both.

### ‚úÖ Easy Reporting
Your monthly review already has all your accomplishments summarized. Generating reports takes 10-15 minutes, not hours.

### ‚úÖ Delegation Support
Scripts and frameworks reduce anxiety. Evidence tracking builds confidence over time.

### ‚úÖ Private vs. Public
- **Private**: `personal/` folder (your raw thoughts and reflections)
- **Work**: `work_tracking/` and `goals/bci_goals/` (internal to BCI)
- **External**: `goals/career_goals/` and `reports/career_portfolio/` (for job search)
